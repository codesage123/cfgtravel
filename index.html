Our Strategic Partners

G-CITI is a skills development organization, working with unemployed people from socially and economically disadvantaged backgrounds in South Africa, Cape Town Area. G-CITI mission is to develop people, especially unemployed people to become part of the growing economy. Within our Training & Development programme, we have maintained over 75% success rate of graduates becoming economically active, on average 15% of these have started their own businesses. The uniqueness of the training is focused on technology and business. The current digital divide that exist in disadvantage communities is addressed by the strategic objectives of G-CITI.
